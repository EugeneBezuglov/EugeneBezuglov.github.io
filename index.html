About Me

After studying data analytics at university, I transitioned to studying business administration and eventually established a retail company. It was small yet successful; I navigated it through the 2014 financial crisis and COVID-19 pandemic and ultimately sold it after 12 years of operation.

Having studied data analytics, I constantly used various tools to make sense of the data I collected when running a business. At some point I started to realize I enjoy working with data more than actually running a business.

Also, during my business career I spent several years working part-time for Index Group, a major Heineken contractor that would specialize in organizing BTL marketing campaigns. I took part in organizing and supervising campaigns at the Men's Hockey World Cup 2016, UEFA Champions League 2016–2018, UEFA Europa League 2016–2018, and at many other events.

That experience showed me that working under a great management team is a blessing. Now I seek to combine my passion for data with working together with a great team, and to deliver as much value as I can, and even more.

If you share similar values, please contact me!
